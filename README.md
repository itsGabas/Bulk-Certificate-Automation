# 🤖 Bulk Certificate Registration Automation (Selenium)

Internal automation project developed to register and update digital certificates across hundreds of companies in a third-party platform, eliminating repetitive manual work.

---

## 🎯 Objective
Automate the process of registering and updating company certificates in a web platform, reducing manual effort and freeing up time for more valuable tasks.

---

## 🧠 Problem
The platform used by the company requires manual registration and updates for each client, one by one.

With nearly 400 companies, the process was:
- Extremely time-consuming  
- Repetitive and exhausting  
- Dependent on human speed and attention  

Completing this task manually could take an entire workday (or more), impacting productivity.

---

## ⚙️ Solution

A Python automation using Selenium was developed to simulate user actions and perform bulk operations directly in the platform.

The script:

- Logs into the platform  
- Navigates through company records  
- Automatically registers or updates certificates  
- Handles repetitive interactions without manual input  

Even though execution may take a few hours, the process runs in the background, allowing other tasks to be performed simultaneously.

---

## 📊 Impact

- Eliminated the need for manual processing of ~400 companies  
- Reduced active human effort from hours to minutes  
- Increased productivity by enabling parallel work  
- Improved consistency and reduced human errors
- Reduced manual workload from ~6–8 hours to a few minutes of supervision.

---

## ⚠️ Limitations

- Challenges with handling pagination dynamically  
- Dependent on stability and structure of the third-party platform
